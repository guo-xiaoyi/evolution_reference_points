Welcome to the Experiment!

Risky Situation Evaluation Task

Welcome to the risky situation evaluation task! This is an online behavioral experiment trying to understand human's behavior facing intertemporal risks in real time settings.

The current task will take you 30-45 (thirty to forty-five) minutes. You can use your mouse to control the panel and select. You can also prepare a piece of paper, a pocket calculator, and a pen.

This expeirment asks you to attend three times. Your continous participation is important to us.

Please click "Start" button if you understand everything on this page and want to participate in this experiment.

How to play

Follow the instructions provided in each section. Make sure to read carefully and complete each task to the best of your ability. If you have any questions, feel free to ask the experimenter using the Prolific chatting window. Work as if this is your real money. It is important for us to elicit your true preferences even though you do not really earn or lose money from the depicted situations you see later.

Who are we

We are researchers from the University of St. Gallen, Switzerland. We cordially invite you to take part in our risky situation evaluation tasks. This experiment is part of our project "Evolution of Reference Points over Time". We are: Xiaoyi Guo, Matthias Weber, Enrico de Giorgi, Andrija Lukovic.

What to expect

You will be presented with various choice situations and asked to make valuations. Your responses will help us understand decision-making processes under uncertainty. We will incentivize you by paying you a show-up fee for three times, and a bonus if you attend all three sessions.
