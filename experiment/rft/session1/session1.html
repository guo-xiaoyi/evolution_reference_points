Artificial intelligence policy

The goal of this experiment is to understand your preferences and behaviors under intertemporal uncertainty scenarios; hence it is very important for you to take the experiment seriously and do not use AI tools to assist you in making decisions during the experiment. This includes directly letting AI to complete the evaluation tasks, providing our materials to AI and ask from which perspective should I think, and asking AI for advice when you are uncertain about a decision that you will make.

There is no strict correct or wrong answers to the questions. Therefore, AI's assistance will not help us understand human behavior better.

We deploy algorithmatic tools powered by Prolific to monitor your behavior during the experiment to detect potential use of AI tools. If we find evidence that you have used AI assistance, we reserve the right to exclude your data from the analysis and withhold any payments associated with the experiment.
