Instructions Overview

In this experiment, you will see several time-variant risky prospects They are risky options that are elapsed over time. In different time periods, according to the previous results, the payoff is different. . Below is an examplary risky prospect. A time-variant risky prospect contains three time sessions When you participate in our experiment once, it counts as a session. We have three sessions at all (so you will need to participate three times.) , that is, starting from now, your final payoff will be determined individually three times and you can know the final result at the end of the third session. Understanding a Time-Variant Risky Prospect

We now explain how to understand this risky prospect. At the current session, you see "Start". This is because so far you have not accumulated any wealth, so your total payoff is 0. You should then look at the next column of the prospect, which has two nodes, marked with "+$10" and "-$10", and a centered fortune wheel. The upper node means that you can have a payoff of $10, which makes your accumulated wealth to $10. There is a connected line between this node and the starting point marked "60%"; this means that you have 60% probabilityThis means that out of 100 cases, 60 cases have a corresponding result. to end up at this node. Analogously, you have a probability of 40% to reach the node "-$10", which means your accumulated wealth will be -$10 (you lose 10 dollars by participating in this risky prospect). The accompanied fortune wheel is a simpler but equivalent illustration of this probability relationship. If you roll this fortune wheel once, the final stop position of the arrow will determine how much do you finally have in this session. If you end up in the darker blue region (the upper panel that is marked with 60%), you will gain $10 immediately; otherwise, you lose $10 immediately. This will happen at the end of Session 1.

After a roll of the fortune wheel, you will end up at one node at the end of Session 1. Suppose this node is +$10. Then you will see a line marked with 100% directly connected to the next node +$7. This means that you have a probability of 100% A probability of 100% means it is a sure thing that one result will happen. Put it in the previous case, it will be that out of 100 cases, 100 (all) cases have a corresponding result. that you can have another 7 dollars in your cumulative wealth. You will then have $17 in your account at the end of Session 2.

Similarly, at the end of Session 2, you will see two lines connected to two nodes at the end of Session 3. The upper line is marked with 80%, which means you have a probability of 80% to gain another $8, making your total wealth $25; the lower line is marked with 20%, which means you have a probability of 20% to gain $0, making your total wealth unchanged to be $17 at the end of Session 3.

Your Payment

Hypothetical choice

Note that your evaluations, choices, and selections to the risky prospects do not matter for your actual payoff, that is, it is hypothetical. However, we request you to evaluate these choice situations as if it is authentic. In this way, we know your true preferences and your opinions to these choice situations.

Our incentivization to you

We provide financial compensation as incentivization to you in a combined way: You first receive a flat show-up fee of $8 three times. Further, if you attend all three sessions and the quality of your response is sufficiently high, we will incentivize you another bonus fee of $8; you will be further listed in a pool of participants, which we will randomly choose three to have an additional financial compensation of $500.

Drop-out

If you drop out before completing all three sessions, you will only receive a flat show-up fee of $8 per session you have attended. For example, if you attend Session 1 and Session 2, but drop out before attending Session 3, you will receive $16 in total. You are also not qualified to receive the bonus fee, or be selected as one of the three to win the additional prize of $500.

To avoid you missing one session, we will send you messages 24 hours and 1 hour before the next session starts.

We unfortunately cannot collect your choices anymore if you missed one session, no matter out of what reason. This is due to our high requirement of periodical interval and strict longitudinal design. Your data will then be deleted and we will pay you according to our incentivization plan.

Experiment Participant ID

In your experiment, we will collect your account information from Prolific (your email address and other fundmanetal demographic information). Further, you will receive an Experiment Participant ID (ExID) that consists of seven codes composed of numbers and capital letters (for example, F7S1G03). The ExID is linked with your Prolific account and is a unique key to retrieve your experimental status across sessions and acquire your additional payment. You will receive your ExID at the end of this session. At the beginning of each subsequent session, you have to enter this ExID to retrieve your experiment, so please keep your ExID in a safe place.

Your ExID will not change across the whole experiment procedure (across 3 sessions). However, if you drop out one session, your ExID will be deactivated and we will pay you according to our incentivization plan.
